Introduction: The Heart of Chamakkal

Nestled comfortably against the breathtaking, lush green backdrop of the Western Ghats, within the hilly village panchayat of Payyavoor in the Kannur district, lies our beloved village of Chamakkal. It is right here that G.L.P.S. Chamakkal has stood as a warm beacon of learning for over six decades.

When we lovingly look back at our humble beginnings in 1957, starting merely as a single-teacher institution, it fills our collective hearts with immense pride to see the vibrant, colorful educational hub we have blossomed into today. We are a joyful second home to our young children.

The Vision: Surakshit Marg

Every single day, we wake up to heartbreaking news of accidents on our roads. Often, the root cause is not a grand mechanical failure, but a fleeting moment of minor negligence. As the number of vehicles multiplies, the risk to our precious children multiplies with it.

This pressing social issue caught the undivided attention of two major societal powerhouses: the Kuttukaran Group and the SCMS Group. Together, they conceptualized "Surakshit Marg" (Safe Path)—a beautifully designed road safety awareness program with a specific target audience: school children.

🚩Project Banner
Crop Photo

Why Start With Schools?

History shows us that a culture of safe driving cannot be aggressively forced onto adults overnight. By meticulously and playfully instilling this traffic culture in our little children from the beginning, we are organically molding a generation that naturally respects the roads.

Chapter 2: Marking Our Territory

Chapter 3: The Crown Jewel

If there is one single aspect of our journey that fills our hearts with absolute joy, it is the creation of 'Kuttikkavala'—our fully functional, beautifully colorful Children's Traffic Junction.

How do you effectively teach a lively five-year-old about the absolute sanctity of a zebra crossing in a way they will never forget? You put them in the driver's seat of a miniature car!

🛣️Kuttikkavala View

We constructed a highly realistic model road system right next to the Varna Koodaram. It features distinct, bright road markings and perfectly spaced black-and-white zebra crossings.

Chapter 4: Pledges & Signatures

The absolute foundation of traffic literacy is understanding road signs. They are the universal language of safety. To ensure our young students became utterly fluent in this language, we made a substantial permanent investment.

Conclusion: The Road Ahead
